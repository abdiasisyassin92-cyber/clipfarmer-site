The pipeline

How ClipFarmer works

Five automated stages, zero manual steps.

1

Scrape audio + background

asset_scraper.py downloads trending phonk / motivational audio and cinematic background footage.

2

Render 9:16 vertical clip

clip_renderer.py combines audio + background, burns title text, hook overlay, and Gumroad CTA via ffmpeg.

3

Approve or auto-post

Discord bot sends a preview card with Approve / Reject buttons — or auto_post=True skips review entirely.

4

Schedule at peak times

scheduler.py queues posts at 7am, 12pm, and 7pm using multi_publisher.py to hit TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram simultaneously.

5

Analyse and optimise

analytics_engine.py pulls view and retention stats and automatically weights better-performing hooks for future clips.

Transparency

Data & Privacy

ClipFarmer is designed with minimal data usage in mind.

What data ClipFarmer accesses via TikTok API

  • Creator profile info (username, display name) — to confirm the correct account is authorised
  • Video publish permission — to upload and post clips on behalf of the creator
  • Post status — to confirm successful publication

What ClipFarmer does NOT do

  • Does not collect, store, or process any third-party user data
  • Does not read follower data, DMs, or comments
  • Does not sell or share any data with third parties
  • Does not serve ads or operate as a multi-user platform
